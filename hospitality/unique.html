About Us



" Ideally located in center of Surat city, on the most convenient approached roads, stand the Unique Hospital – Multispeciality & Research Institute, 100 bedded hospital with 32 bedded well equipped ICU. Inaugurated for care of the patients on December 30, 2012, the hospital opened its doors for the service of healing with humanity. The hospital is run by Surat Lifecare Pvt Ltd."

" It was the culmination of a noble desire by Unique Hospital Family, to build a hospital that would gratify to the fundamental healthcare needs of the common man with aim to provide quality health care services."
